e-Parliament Programme: IT for parliamentary work

The e-Parliament Programme dates back to 2020 and it aims at providing enhanced legislative and parliamentary services to MEPs through innovative state-of-the-art ICT tools and solutions that increase parliament’s openness, accessibility and effectiveness..

OVERVIEW

eParliament is an end-to-end enterprise solution which helps the State and Citizens benefit from the use of technology in the government sector. It provides a paperless digital solution to automate the day-to-day events of the Parliament and Legislative assembly. eParliament solutions provide maximum benefits that have an operational, environmental, social and economic impact.

Objectives Of Work Process

Information and communication technology requirements of the Parliament are managed by the Department of Information Systems & Management. The department was added to the long-established seven departments of the Sri Lankan Parliament in late 2004.
